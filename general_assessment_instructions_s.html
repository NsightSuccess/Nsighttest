General Assessment Instructions

Thank you, in advance, for your participation in this assessment process.

To change a response, simply make a new selection and the original selection will be eliminated.

Please attempt to answer each question rather than leaving any blank. If none of the choices presented are completely satisfactory, choose one that you believe is most nearly correct. During the timed sections, if you skip a question, you can scroll back to the question if time remains on the clock. Once time expires, responses will automatically be submitted.

When completing an un-timed section, you will be able to return to any question you left blank by using your scroll bar.

NOTE: To return to any question (timed or un-timed), you may only use the scroll bar. DO NOT use the backspace as this will result with your answers being prematurely submitted. You can not go back to a section once your answers are submitted.

It is best to respond to the un-timed personality questions in the manner that best reflects how you perceive yourself in a work environment. Please remain true to your own individuality. Although time is not limited, it is best to work as rapidly as is comfortable for you. It is best to go with your first thought, rather than overanalyzing the questions. Please do not leave an answer blank, since to do so may skew the results more so than an occasional uncertain response.

Please do not discuss your answers with anyone while taking the NSight assessment. Avoid distractions as much as possible and please complete the questionnaire at one sitting.

Please do not utilize outside resources such as dictionaries or calculators. However, you may utilize scrap paper.

If you lose your connection while taking the NSight assessment, please contact us for assistance.

Please do not refresh your browser during an assessment since the result will be the loss of your responses.
