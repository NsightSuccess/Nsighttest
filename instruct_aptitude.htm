Aptitude Testing Instructions

Thank you in advance for your participation in this assessment process.

You will be presented the same questions on each of the timed NSight questionnaires that other individuals are invited to complete.

You will encounter the following formats: multiple-choice, true false, numeric answers, and matching items.

You will be invited to take a short practice test prior to completing each section. The purpose for these practice tests is to familiarize you with the various computer display formats, assessment question formats and the response methods to be used during the course of the assessment.

You will be presented four timed questionnaires for completion. These questionnaires include a 30-item Verbal Reasoning, 15-item Numerical Reasoning, 25-item Vocabulary and a 50-item Visual Perception section. You may expect this process to take approximately an hour. However, since this portion of the assessment is delivered in four timed sections, if necessary, you will be able to break at the end of each section.

The first timed event will be the Verbal Reasoning questionnaire. Once you have taken the practice test for this questionnaire you will be invited to complete the actual timed assessment. Please prepare to take this assessment without interruption. Your period of confinement will be less than 20 minutes. Once a timed section has begun, it cannot be stopped. Please click here to go to the Verbal Reasoning portion of this questionnaire.

The second timed event will be the Numerical Reasoning questionnaire. Once you have taken the practice test for this questionnaire you will be invited to complete the actual timed assessment. Please prepare to take this assessment without interruption. Your period of confinement will be less than 20 minutes. Once a timed section has begun, it cannot be stopped. Please click here to go to the Numerical Reasoning portion of this questionnaire.

The third timed event will be the Vocabulary questionnaire. Once you have taken the practice test for this questionnaire you will be invited to complete the actual timed assessment. Please prepare to take this assessment without interruption. Your period of confinement will be less than 15 minutes. Once a timed section has begun, it cannot be stopped. Please click here to go to the Vocabulary portion of this questionnaire.

The forth and final timed event will be the Visual Perception exercise. Once you have taken the practice test for this portion of the assessment, you will be invited to complete the actual timed assessment. Please prepare to take this assessment without interruption. Your period of confinement will be less than 5 minutes. Once a timed section has begun, it cannot be stopped. Please click here to go to the Visual Perception portion of this questionnaire.
